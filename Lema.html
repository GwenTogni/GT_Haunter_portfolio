Coat of arm of Lema

Coat of arm for the new municipality of Lema.
Contest currently underway.

Concept

The coat of arms depicts a wolf on the top of a mountain.
It is based on a local legend, where this animal saved a child from a fire.
The red background rapresent the flames.
The other variant represents the sunny climate, and a chestnut, a typical fruit of the place.
